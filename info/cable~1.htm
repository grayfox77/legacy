F1 Spirit 3D Special - Cable

Konami's RA 005, F1 Spirit 3D Special, incorporates a battle mode. Two MSX'es can be connected to one another via a special cable. This cable was sold seperately. I have never seen the real thing, so I've gathered this information from hacking the game. If you do have it, could you please compare it to this.

Note that F1 Spirit 3D Special is an MSX2+ game, so you'll need two MSX2+ computers or higher to use this cable. You'll also need two copies of the game (or two copies of disk B at least).

The cable connects the MSX'es via the game ports. I must be put in port B of both computers. It is quite a simple interface.

First of all, before two MSX'es can communicate, the grounds must be connected. (connect pin 9 to pin 9.) The cable uses seperate connections for communication every way. The three outputs are connected to three inputs both ways. (connect pin 6 to 1, pin 7 to 2, pin 8 to 3 and vice versa.)
