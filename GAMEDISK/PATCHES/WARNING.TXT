TRANSLAT.COM is an utility created for patching original Japanese Games.
Be careful when using TRANSLAT.COM   IT DOESN'T WORK ON MSXDOS2.
You may loose data on your HD if using TRANSLAT.COM when a HD attached.

For the correct use of TRANSLAT.COM copy the files to a boot diskette, then
startup from diskette and execute TRANSLAT GAME.TRD, where GAME.TRD is the
patch to be applied.
