=======================================
       MERITUULEN KARAISEMA 
=======================================
 by Bandwagon 1998

 - MSX1/PSG/VDP1/DOS1/64k/16k required
   and recommended.

 To run:
   Insert disk and type "MERI" at the
  command prompt. Boot with CTRL to 
  remove unnecessary diskdrivers in 
  DOS1.
