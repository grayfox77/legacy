Introduction
Guide your character through 12 intrincated mazes. Can you reach the
goal?.
Karoshi Corp. brings you the classic game form SEGA with the same
feeling and levels. Graphics, music and gameplay were recreated from
the original Sega Master System game for you to enjoy them in your MSX.
This game was Eduardo A. Robsy Petrus' example project at MSXdev'04
contest.

Controls
Use cursor keys or joystick to control your little character.

Connect the EAR jack of your MSX to the PHONES entry of your CD Player. Adjust the volume of
your CD Player to aprox. 3/4. Type BLOAD "CAS:",R and press RETURN. Press PLAY on your CD
Player. The game will start loading. If not, try adjusting the volume again and repeat the process.
When a disk drive is present, remember to press SHIFT key while booting the computer.
